REMOTE WORK POLICY - ACME CORPORATION

OVERVIEW
ACME Corporation supports flexible work arrangements to promote work-life balance and productivity.

ELIGIBILITY
- Employees must have completed 90-day probationary period
- Role must be suitable for remote work
- Manager approval required
- Must maintain reliable internet connection and appropriate workspace

REMOTE WORK OPTIONS

1. FULL-TIME REMOTE
- Work from home 5 days per week
- Required to attend quarterly in-person meetings
- Must be available during core business hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time)

2. HYBRID SCHEDULE
- Minimum 2 days per week in office
- Flexible scheduling with manager approval
- Must attend all mandatory in-person meetings

3. OCCASIONAL REMOTE
- Work from home as needed with advance notice
- No formal approval process required
- Maximum 2 days per week

EQUIPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY
- Company provides laptop, monitor, and necessary software
- Employees responsible for internet connection
- IT support available for company-provided equipment
- VPN required for accessing company systems

COMMUNICATION EXPECTATIONS
- Available on Slack during work hours
- Video camera on for team meetings
- Respond to emails within 4 hours during business hours
- Update calendar with availability status

SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
- Use company VPN for all work activities
- Do not share passwords or access credentials
- Lock computer when away from desk
- Report any security incidents immediately
- No public WiFi for accessing sensitive data

WORKSPACE REQUIREMENTS
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Ergonomic setup to prevent injury
- Adequate lighting and ventilation
- Professional background for video calls

PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS
- Same productivity standards as in-office work
- Meet all deadlines and deliverables
- Participate actively in team meetings
- Maintain regular communication with manager and team

TIME TRACKING
- Log hours in company time tracking system
- Take regular breaks (lunch and short breaks)
- No expectation to work outside normal hours
- Overtime must be pre-approved

TERMINATION OF REMOTE WORK
- Company reserves right to modify or terminate remote work arrangements
- 30-day notice will be provided when possible
- Performance issues may result in return to office requirement
