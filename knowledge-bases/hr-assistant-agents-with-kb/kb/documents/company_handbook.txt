COMPANY HANDBOOK - ACME CORPORATION

Welcome to ACME Corporation! This handbook contains essential information about our company policies and culture.

WORK HOURS
- Standard work hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Flexible work arrangements available with manager approval
- Core hours (required presence): 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

TIME OFF POLICY
- Vacation Days: 15 days per year for employees with 0-5 years of service
- Vacation Days: 20 days per year for employees with 5+ years of service
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Personal Days: 3 days per year
- Holidays: 10 company-observed holidays

BENEFITS
- Health Insurance: Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage
- 401(k) Retirement Plan: Company matches up to 5% of salary
- Life Insurance: Company-provided coverage equal to 2x annual salary
- Professional Development: $2,000 annual budget for training and conferences

CODE OF CONDUCT
- Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Report any ethical concerns to HR or management
- Follow all safety protocols and procedures

DRESS CODE
- Business casual attire Monday through Thursday
- Casual Friday (jeans and company-branded shirts acceptable)
- Professional attire required for client meetings

COMMUNICATION
- Check email at least twice daily during work hours
- Respond to urgent messages within 2 hours
- Use Slack for team communication
- Schedule meetings through Outlook calendar

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
- Annual performance reviews conducted in December
- Mid-year check-ins in June
- Continuous feedback encouraged throughout the year
- Performance tied to annual bonus and salary adjustments
